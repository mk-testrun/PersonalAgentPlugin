## Erzeugt

- `ComponentName.razor` (Markup + `@rendermode`)
- `ComponentName.razor.cs` (Code-Behind, partial class)
- `ComponentName.razor.css` (CSS-Isolation)
- `ComponentNameTest.cs` (bUnit-Test-Skeleton)
- Resx-Einträge für alle UI-Strings
